OUR IMPACT

Our team is dedicated to creating a world where everyone has a voice, through awareness, equity, and creativity.

Our Impact

AAHYF Donation
C.A.R.E donated school supplies to AAYHF to support their mission. AAHYF provides hands-on service through school and community-based programs, enhancing the quality of life for African Americans and low-income youth and families.

Collaborative Research & Writing

Our Research & Writing Volunteering Department has contributed to two published research projects, focusing on discrimination and struggles minorities face on a daily basis

Cultural Surprises Program

Our ongoing Cultural Surprises initiative empowers volunteers to create and deliver arts and crafts for minority families and individuals. These handmade gifts celebrate culture, spread joy, and promote cross-cultural understanding.
